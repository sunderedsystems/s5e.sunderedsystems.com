# Proficiency

- Characters start at proficiency +2.

### Progression

- +2 at levels 1, 5, 9, 13
- +1 at level 17 (max +9)
    - Gain proficiency in a third (final) ability at level 20.

## Proficiency Table

| Level | Proficiency Bonus   |
|-------|---------------------|
| 1     | +2                 |
| 5     | +4                 |
| 9     | +6                 |
| 13    | +8                 |
| 17    | +9                 |
| 20    | (add 3rd ability)  |
